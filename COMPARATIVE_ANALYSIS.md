# Comparative Architecture Analysis: Public Blockchain Aid vs. CBDC Direct Distribution

*UN Technical Briefing - Draft*

This document provides a comparative analysis of two digital aid distribution models for consideration by the UN working group.

## Analysis Criteria

The comparison will be structured around five key criteria:

1. Transaction Throughput & Latency
2. Privacy & Data Sovereignty
3. Cost of Transfer & Economic Viability
4. Security & Custodial Model
5. Governance & Interoperability
